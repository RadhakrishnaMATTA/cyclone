Flood Precautions

Precaution Details
Stay Informed Monitor weather forecasts and flood alerts regularly through local authorities and the Flood Detection System.
Prepare an Emergency Kit Ensure your emergency kit includes essentials such as food, water, medicines, and important documents.
Avoid Flooded Areas Do not attempt to cross flooded roads or enter floodwaters, as they may hide hazards like debris or strong currents.
Evacuate if Necessary If authorities issue an evacuation order, leave the area immediately and seek shelter in a safe location.
