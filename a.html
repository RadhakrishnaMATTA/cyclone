Cyclone Prediction and Precautions

What is a Cyclone?

A cyclone is a large-scale air mass that rotates around a strong center of low atmospheric pressure. Cyclones are caused by warm ocean waters, high humidity in the atmosphere, and converging winds.

How Cyclones Form

  • Warm ocean water heats the air above it, causing it to rise.
  • This rising air creates a low-pressure area near the ocean surface.
  • As more warm air rises, surrounding air moves in to replace it, creating strong winds.
  • When these winds begin to rotate, a cyclone forms.
